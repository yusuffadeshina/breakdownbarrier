Empowering and inspiring individuals

Break Down Barriers is dedicated to keeping young people in Bristol engaged through personalised mentoring programmes.
We believe in empowering every young person, regardless of their background or circumstances, to unlock their fullest potential.
Our mission is to inspire, support, and transform lives by breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for growth and success
